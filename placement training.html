Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of placement assistance do you provide?

We offer end-to-end placement assistance, including resume building, interview preparation (technical and HR), mock interviews, aptitude training, and connecting you with companies hiring for relevant roles. Our support continues until you successfully secure a job.

Are the trainers industry experienced?

Yes, all our trainers are seasoned industry professionals with extensive experience in their respective domains. They bring real-world insights and practical knowledge into the classroom, ensuring you get the best possible training.

What is the duration of the placement training programs?

The duration varies depending on the specific course and your individual needs. Our programs typically range from 4 weeks to 12 weeks, with flexible scheduling options including weekend batches and evening classes.

Do you guarantee job placement?

While we don't offer a 100% job guarantee due to various external factors, we provide comprehensive placement support, connect you with our network of hiring partners, and work tirelessly to maximize your chances of getting placed. Our strong track record speaks for itself.

What are the eligibility criteria for joining your programs?

Eligibility varies by program. Generally, a basic understanding of computer fundamentals is beneficial. Specific technical courses may have prerequisites, which will be detailed in the course description. We welcome both freshers and experienced professionals looking to upskill.
